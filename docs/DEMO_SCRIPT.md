# Demo Script

Use this sequence for a university/project presentation.

## 1. Dashboard

Open Home.

Explain:

- Active inventory count.
- Total signed agreements.
- Closed deals.
- Potential commission.
- Agreements ending soon.
- Today's tasks.
- Pipeline phase distribution.

## 2. Inventory

Open Inventory.

Show:

- Search by customer, code, phone, location, or agent.
- Expiring soon filter.
- Exclusive and rent filters.
- More filters for agent, source, transaction, agreement type, and phase.
- Property cards with agreement code, customer, agent, phase, expiry, and commission.

## 3. Property Details

Tap View details.

Show:

- Customer/contact data.
- Property and agreement information.
- Agreement expiry status.
- Workflow timeline.
- Related tasks.
- Potential or confirmed commission.

## 4. Workflow Phase Movement

Tap Advance phase.

Explain:

- The property moves through the resale workflow.
- Phase history records the movement.
- Progress percentage updates.

## 5. Closing A Deal

Tap Close deal.

Explain:

- Status becomes closed.
- Phase moves to closing and commission received.
- Commission becomes confirmed.

## 6. Add Agreement

Open Add.

Show:

- Agent assignment.
- Market and transaction type.
- Customer and property details.
- Agreement type and source.
- Auto-calculated 3-month end date.
- Required-field validation.

## 7. Schedule

Open Schedule.

Show:

- Open follow-ups.
- Overdue/today priority badges.
- Add task form.
- Mark done action.

## 8. Reports

Open Reports.

Show:

- Biweekly signed agreements.
- Closed deals.
- Confirmed and potential commission.
- Agent production.
- Pipeline phase distribution.
- Team totals.
- Generate Biweekly Report action.
- Export-ready summary text.

## 9. Team Leader Tracking

Open Team.

Show:

- Team selection.
- Team inventory.
- Closed deals.
- Commission.
- Agent target progress.
- Exclusive listings.
- Follow-up load.

## 10. Reset Demo Data

Open Settings.

Show:

- Demo data counts.
- Seed sample data.
- Reset demo data.
- Role demo explanation.
